Pick any color, preview it live on the canvas, then click Generate. New variant inherits slot, offset, scale, and rarity from c1 and can itself be re-tinted later from its own Variants tab.
Per-color palette swap. Loads the source's idle frame, scans every opaque pixel color, gives you a swatch per unique color. Pick new colors via the color pickers — preview updates live. On Save, the server applies the swaps to every frame across every animation of the source, then ships the variant as a new cosmetic. Works for both static (Quick Add) and animated (c1–c43 hand-drawn) sources.
